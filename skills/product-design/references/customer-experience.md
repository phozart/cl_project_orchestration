## Experience Metrics

### Leading Indicators
Track these early - they predict success/failure:

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| DAU/MAU | Daily active / Monthly active | > 0.2 |
| Time to Value | Minutes to first "aha" moment | < 5 min |
| Activation Rate | % completing key actions | > 60% |
| Customer Effort Score | How easy to use (1-7) | > 5.5 |

### Lagging Indicators
Track these later - they confirm success/failure:

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Net Promoter Score | Promoters - Detractors | > 30 |
| CSAT | Customer satisfaction % | > 80% |
| Churn Rate | % leaving per month | < 5% |
| Lifetime Value | Revenue per customer | > 3x CAC |

## Experience Principles

Define 3-5 principles that guide all design decisions:

| Principle | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Speed First | Every interaction under 200ms | Instant feedback on actions |
| Progressive Disclosure | Show only what's needed | Advanced settings hidden |
| Delight in Details | Micro-interactions matter | Satisfying button animations |
